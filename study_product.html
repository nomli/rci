Technical Info

A cyanoacrylate adhesive that bonds with incredible speed and strength on a wide variety of substrates; impact, water and heat resistant (up to 120°C). Color: Pale yellow

How to Use

Apply in drops to one surface only. Press parts together and hold firmly for a few seconds. Good contact is essential. Use Mighty Remover to clean up or de-bond newly cured instant glue. Wipe nozzle with apiece of cloth before replacing the cap.

Uses

Typically used for bonding porous and non-porous materials that require a fast and strong fix.

Features

  • Setting time: 26-30 seconds

Availability

Available in 3g tube
